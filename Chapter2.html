Chapter2

Fighters from the planet Earth are preparing for the main Tournament on Mars. First, they must pass the qualifications and be among the top 10 to take part in the tournament. Five regions from which will emerge two best fighters are: Europe, Asia, Africa, America and Australia. The rules are simple : warriors will fight until someone will give up or will not be able to fight.

Qualifying Tournament which was made on the Earth went fast. President of the Earth from over 10k people choose 10 magnificent whom can send on a journey after the dream. Fighters are happy but they are not fully aware of what surprises wait for them on another planet. The tournament will take place in approx half a year so this time is dedicated for the preparation in the special resort prepared by the president.
